Richard Nosan, President

A lifelong Clevelander, Richard Nosan was educated at Miami University in Oxford, Ohio, and received his business training at IBM's legendary Executive School. From 1977 to 1986, he specialized in industrial sales and ran the office leasing division at Ostendorf-Morris, then the most substantial commercial real estate firm in Ohio and one of the largest of its kind in the country. In 1986 Rick left Ostendorf-Morris to pursue real estate development and investment through Property Advisors Group, which has grown thanks to his keen foresight, wide contacts, and deep knowledge of the Cleveland real estate market.

Real Estate Development

In 1986 PAG conceived and developed Rockside Business Point, the first flexible office-industrial buildings in suburban Cleveland’s premier Rockside Road business district. We expanded our portfolio in this submarket by developing the five buildings that comprise the adjacent High Pointe Corporate Park, designed by award-winning architect Steven Kordalski between 1990 and 2001. PAG continues to own, manage, and improve these properties and others even as we pursue new investment and development possibilities in Ohio and throughout the country.

Property Management

PAG’s approach has been to increase our properties’ revenue and the return on our investment though smart, focused management. While we watch every expense, we’re also intensely proud of our buildings, improving them proactively and leasing them aggressively even as we focus on retaining valued tenants. Our seasoned management team is passionate about customer service and specializes in creating strong, transparent relationships with tenants that lead to mutual satisfaction and success.with tenants that lead to mutual satisfaction and success.

Project Management

PAG specializes in managing tenant-improvement projects for clients in our own properties and throughout the region. Our team of expert architects, engineers, and subcontractors—many of whom have partnered with us for decades—allows us to identify our clients’ space needs, plan strategically, and work quickly to move from blueprint to final finish. Experienced project managers ensure quality construction on time, overseeing the competitive bidding process, supervising subcontractors’ work, and implementing both cost-saving measures and creative approaches to scheduling.

Acquisitions

“PAG has successfully developed and bought over $70 million of real estate, and we continue to build our portfolio by acquiring value-add properties that complement and diversify our core holdings.In recent years, we have purchased Southport Center, a 96,000 square-foot high-tech office building on Rockside Road in Valley View, Ohio; raw land in Columbus, Ohio, that has since been developed for hotel use; and a 333,600 square-foot net-lease office- laboratory in Indianapolis, Indiana. We’re currently looking to acquire class A and B office and office-flex properties with sale prices between $5 and $50 million.
